How to use

1
Add photos

Drag image files into the Photos panel on the left, or click it to browse. Photos land in your library and can be reused across multiple cells. HEIC/HEIF is supported.

2
Place photos

Drag a photo from the library and drop it onto any bento cell. Drop a different photo on the same cell to swap it out.

3
Resize cells

Click a cell to select it, then drag the corner handle (bottom-right) to expand or shrink it. Use the Width / Height +/− controls in the right panel for precise column and row spans.

4
Pan & zoom a photo

Click a filled cell to select it. Drag inside the cell to pan the photo. Scroll over the cell to zoom. Fine-tune with the sliders in the right panel.

5
Canvas size & color

Use the Canvas dropdown in the top bar to set an aspect ratio — 1:1, 4:5, 9:16, 16:9 — or pick Custom and enter your own dimensions in px, in, or cm. The color swatch next to it sets the canvas background color.

6
Style a cell

With a cell selected, use the right panel to change its background color, corner radius, and shape (rounded, square, circle, squircle, diamond, hex). The Gap and Corners sliders in the top bar apply globally.

7
Delete & undo

Click the × on any cell to remove it, or select a cell and press Delete / Backspace. Press Cmd/Ctrl + Z to undo any change — up to 40 steps back.

8
Load a project

Use Load ↑ in the Photos panel to restore a previously saved .bb project file — photos, layout, canvas size, and all.

9
Export

Click Export Image ↓ to download your bento box as PNG, JPEG, or WebP. The canvas size you set in the nav pre-fills the export dimensions. Scale up to 4× for high-res or print output.
